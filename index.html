    Discover OUR HISTORY

    Pasta Paradise is an Indian restaurant in Rajahmundry,EG Dist . Embark on a gastronomic journey around the flavors of overseas. Our chef offers a variety of pastas, risottos but also raclette.

    WHAT you will taste at our place :

    • Pasta with Parmigiano (White Sauce & Red Sauce).
    • Risottos.
    • Salads.
    • Raclette.

    Choose your pasta (Spaghetti, Penne, Farfalle, Tagliatelle) and it is served after dipping in a wheel of parmigiano.
